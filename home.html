Meteor

**Kim “Meteor” Tae-o** is a professional *Valorant* player from South Korea, known for his sharp aim, explosive entry fragging, and standout performances in the **Pacific region**. As a duelist main, Meteor has built a reputation for his high mechanical skill on agents like **Jett** and **Raze**, often creating space for his team with fearless, high-impact plays. He rose to prominence with **Gen.G Esports**, where he became one of the team’s stars during their impressive **VCT Pacific 2024** run — including a strong showing at international events that put both him and his team on the global radar. Meteor’s aggressive playstyle, combined with his clutch potential and composure, has made him one of Korea’s most exciting and promising *Valorant* talents.

Derke

**Nikita “Derke” Sirmitev** isn’t just a machine with perfect aim — he’s a guy who lives and breathes *Valorant*. Behind the sharp flicks and crazy highlight clips, there’s someone who genuinely loves the grind, the teamwork, and the rush of competition. Growing up in Finland, he turned his passion for gaming into a career that’s inspired players all over the world. With **Fnatic**, Derke’s become known not only for his flashy plays on **Jett** and **Raze**, but also for the calm confidence he brings to every match. He celebrates with heart, takes losses personally, and keeps pushing to be better every day. It’s that mix of fiery talent and human drive — the late-night practice, the pressure, the joy of winning with friends — that makes Derke more than just a great player; he’s someone people can really root for.

Meiy

**Ibuki “Meiy” Seki** is a Japanese *Valorant* pro who’s rapidly earned a reputation as one of the most mechanically gifted players in the world. Known for his blistering aim, aggressive confidence, and sharp decision-making, Meiy has become a cornerstone of the **DetonatioN FocusMe** roster and a standout duelist in the **VCT Pacific** league. His performances in 2025, including posting one of the **highest average combat scores (ACS) in the world**, have proven that he can hang with — and often outshine — the game’s biggest names. But what makes Meiy special isn’t just the stats; it’s his fearless style and calm composure under pressure. Whether he’s dashing into a site on **Jett** or clutching a 1v3, Meiy plays with a mix of discipline and flair that’s electrified fans and inspired a new wave of Japanese players to chase the global stage.
